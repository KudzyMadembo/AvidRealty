The city is ripe with investment opportunities in this market. New York City is one of the most famous and largest cities in the world, but wIth the popularity comes the expense of living there, which might make you believe there isn’t a lot of room to get a return on an investment. This, however, is not true. The following are some ways real estate investors in NYC make money, and they are avenues you can adopt yourself. Buy a Property Directly Investing in a turnkey or fixer-upper property gives you the option of buying a home, fixing it up if need be and renting it out. Keep in mind, the demand is high for purchasing properties in NYC, but there are many companies with a mission to sell these types of properties. You might want to consider a property management company to help with your investment, especially if you don’t actually live in NYC. In any case, there are many real estate investors in NYC who go this route. Real Estate Investment Trust Through a real estate investment trust, or REIT, one can join a group of people as a part of a company looking to invest in the NYC real estate market. REITs gives real estate investors in NYC the ability to invest in commercial properties, residential properties and mortgage loans. You can have access to these types of properties through purchasing stock directly on an open exchange or investing in a mutual fund that concentrates exclusively in public real estate.
